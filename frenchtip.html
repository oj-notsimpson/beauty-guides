How to do Micro French Tips

Products

  • Nail file
  • Nail buffer
  • Base coat
  • Nude or light pink nail polish
  • Tape or French tip stencils
  • Top coat
  • Cuticle oil
  • Pointed cotton swabs and nail polish remover for clean-up

Steps

  1. Clean and Shape Your Nails.
  2. Buff and Prep the Nail Plate.
  3. Apply Base Color.
  4. Add Tape.
  5. Paint the Tip of the Nail.
  6. Round Out the Line.
  7. Top Your Manicure Off With a Sheer Neutral Shade.
  8. Finish With a Thin Layer of Top Coat.
Maintain your nails

As with any manicure, be sure to play it carefully before putting your hands to work. Depending on the formula of your polish and your top coat, it can take several hours for your nails to dry completely.
