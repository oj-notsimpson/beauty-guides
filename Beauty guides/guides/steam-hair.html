How to steam hair without a hooded dryer

Products

  • Conditioner
  • 2 Shower caps
  • Wash cloth

Steps

  1. Smooth conditioner over your hair.
  2. Place a shower cap on your head.
  3. Wet a wash cloth and wring it out.
  4. Put the wash cloth into the microwave for 2 minutes.
  5. Place the face cloth on top of the shower cap.
  6. Put another shower cap over the face cloth.
  7. Leave your hair to steam for 30 minutes.
  8. Rinse your hair with cold water.
Enjoy your hydrated hair!

The beautiful moisturised feeling will last about 1 week. Avoid steaming your hair more often than this, as steaming too often weakens your hair. Leave your hair to air-dry. This maximises the moisture in your hair and minimises heat damage.
